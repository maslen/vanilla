Laminated Bags

Laminated means layered. A laminated bag is therefore made up of different layers of material which are chosen for their individual properties.

Most laminated bags consist of PET or OPP which is the outer layer. This plastic material gives very good printability, total or partial matt finish and a high or medium melting temperature.

The inner layer usually consists of PE which is thermosealable, has good hot tack for large bags, a combination of LLDPE/MDPE, white content for opacity and coextrusion.

The inner and outer layers are laminated together to produce a reel of printed film which can be made into pre-formed bags or supplied on the reel to a V.F.F.S. Packaging Machine.

There are many different types of laminated bags, in all shapes and sizes. Our most popular is the Four Corner Seal, or Stabillo Bag.

Repeat lengths from 130 mm to 1400 mm.

Widths from 60 mm to 425 mm.

Features include:

  • Reseal - Zipper / Slider
  • Tear Notches / Laser Cut
  • One Way Valve / Micro-Perforations / Labyrinth Air Release
  • Gusset Handle
  • K Seal / Glue Dot
