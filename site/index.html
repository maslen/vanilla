Assaye Packaging Ltd (APL) are one of the U.K.'s leading suppliers of High Quality Plastic Laminated Bags, Pouches and Printed Film.

APL's product range features sizes from 50 gm up to 15 kg.

Main areas of business are:

  • Pet Food - dogs, cats and small animals
  • Aquatic Food
  • Human Food
  • Bird Food
  • Fishing Baits
  • Horticultural Products
